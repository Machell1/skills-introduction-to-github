Case Classifications (6.3)
6.3.1 Case Open
A case that is actively under investigation.
6.3.2 Case Suspended
A case put on hold after all investigative leads have been exhausted.
6.3.3 Case Cleared
When charge(s) laid, suspect dead, warrant issued, extradition denied, victim won't prosecute, Coroners Court, Child Diversion, suspect summoned, or report contrived.
6.3.4 Case Closed
When charge(s) preferred (no other suspect), suspect dead (no other suspect), or as ACP CIB deems necessary.
6.3.5 Cold Case
Unsolved and inactive for 3 years after declared suspended.
