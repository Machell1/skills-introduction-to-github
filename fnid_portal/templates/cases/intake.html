Legal Reference Guide — Firearms Act 2022 & Dangerous Drugs Act
Firearms (Prohibition, Restriction & Regulation) Act, 2022:
  • s.5 — Possession of Prohibited Weapon (15yr min; 25yr max)
  • s.6 — Stockpiling (3+ firearms or 50+ rounds; 15yr min)
  • s.7 — Trafficking (20yr min; life max)
  • s.10 — Dealing (20+ rounds deemed dealing; 20yr min)
  • s.35 — Unauthorised Possession of Firearm
  • s.36 — Unauthorised Possession of Ammunition
  • s.42 — Use of Firearm in Commission of Offence
  • s.43 — Shooting with Intent to do GBH
All FA offences — mandatory Gun Court referral
Dangerous Drugs Act (as amended 2015):
  • Part IIIA — Ganja:
  • s.7A — Import/Export Ganja (5yr–life)
  • s.7B — Dealing, Cultivation, Transporting
  • s.7C — Possession (≤2oz ticket J$500; >2oz criminal)
  • Part IV — Cocaine, Heroin:
  • s.8 — Import/Export (10yr–life)
  • s.8A — Selling, Dealing, Transporting (up to 35yr)
  • s.8B — Possession (any cocaine; >1/10oz heroin = deemed dealing)
Deemed Dealing Thresholds:
  • Ganja: >2oz = criminal; 5+ plants = trafficking
  • Cocaine: any quantity = criminal offence
  • Heroin: >1/10oz = deemed dealing
  • Ammunition: 20+ rounds = deemed dealing (FA s.10)
  • Firearms: 3+ = stockpiling (FA s.6)
Court Jurisdiction:
  • All firearm offences — Gun Court
  • Drug summary — Parish Court
  • Drug indictable — Circuit Court
